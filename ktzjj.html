Pengfei Duan received his Ph.D. from the Institute of Chemistry, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2011, with Professor Minghua Liu. From November 2011 to November 2013, he worked as JSPS post-doctoral research fellow at Kyushu University in the laboratory of Professor Nobuo Kimizuka. From December 2013 to October 2015, he was employed by the Center for Molecular System at Kyushu University, served as a teaching assistant. In November 2015, he joined the National Center for Nanoscience and Technology, Key Laboratory of Nanosystem and Hierarchical Fabrication, CAS. His research field is molecular/nano photochemistry and physics.



About us：

我们课题组是一个充满朝气的实验化学研究小组。研究聚焦于多级次的分子、超分子以及纳微米体系中的光学及光化学与物理过程的研究。涉及到多级次组装体系中手性信息的转移传递、激发态能量的转移转换以及手性与能量的协同作用。研究方向包括光子频率上转换材料的开发及其应用拓展、手性光电材料与器件、纳米材料的光化学与光物理研究 以及软物质体系手性科学问题。



Our research group is a vibrant experimental chemistry research group. Our researches focus on optics, photochemistry and physical processes in hierarchical molecular, supramolecular, and nano or micron systems. It involves the transfer of chiral information, the transfer and conversion of excited state energy, and the synergy between chirality and energy in the hierarchical assembled system. Research directions include the development and application of photon upconversion materials, chiral optoelectronic materials and devices, photochemical and photophysical research of nanomaterials, and chiral science of soft matter systems.
