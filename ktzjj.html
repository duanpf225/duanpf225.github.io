"We explore the secrets of chirality"

About us：



Our research group is a vibrant experimental chemistry research group. Our researches focus on optics, photochemistry and physical processes in hierarchical molecular, supramolecular, and nano or micron systems.

It involves the transfer of chiral information, the transfer and conversion of excited state energy, and the synergy between chirality and energy in the hierarchical assembled system.

Research directions include the development and application of photon upconversion materials, chiral optoelectronic materials and devices, photochemical and photophysical research of nanomaterials, and chiral science of soft matter systems.
